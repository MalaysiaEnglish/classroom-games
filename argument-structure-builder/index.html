Argument Structure Builder

Master the Structure of a Formal Argument

In this game, you'll build three arguments from scratch. Each argument has a specific structure that makes it persuasive and effective.

How to Play

  1. Read the scrambled sentences at the top of each round
  2. Drag each sentence to the position where it belongs in the argument
  3. Think about structure: What makes a strong opening? How do you build your case? When should you address the other side?
  4. Click "Check My Answer" to see which sentences are in the right place
  5. Learn and progress through 3 different arguments

You can drag sentences back to the bank or to different slots to reorganise your answer.
